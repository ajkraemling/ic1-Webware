About Blue Jeans Pizza

Cheese Pizza Is What We Do

Since 1980 Blue Jeans has been a Worcester tradition. Our famous one cheese, two cheese, and four cheese thin crust pizza has been awarded Worcester’s Best Plain Pizza multiple times. The roots of our recipe and hand-stretched dough go back to Boston’s North End. And the limited menu means you’ll get a consistent experience every time.
